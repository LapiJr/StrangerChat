Our story

From Naples with love

Restaurant DeLuca began as a modest trattoria opened by siblings Alessandra and Marco DeLuca in 1978. Their dream was simple: bring the warmth of their Neapolitan upbringing to the bustling streets of The Hague. Today, the dream lives on as a modern dining destination where heritage meets innovation.

Our philosophy

Elegance, passion, authenticity

Seasonal artistry

We work directly with local farmers and fishmongers to craft menus that change with the rhythm of the seasons. Sustainability is woven into every plate.

Hospitality first

Every guest is greeted as famiglia. Our team choreographs each service around your preferences, from lighting to playlist to after-dinner digestivo.

Culture & community

We collaborate with Dutch artists, host intimate concerts, and champion female winemakers. DeLuca is more than a restaurant — it is a gathering place.

Milestones

A legacy spanning four decades

1978

DeLuca opens its doors on Spuistraat with a wood-fired oven and twelve tables.

1995

The Velvet Lounge is introduced, blending live jazz with Italian aperitivo culture.

2010

Our wine cellar earns the prestigious Gambero Rosso award for excellence.

2024

Restaurant DeLuca undergoes a renaissance, unveiling a reimagined dining room and modern kitchen.
